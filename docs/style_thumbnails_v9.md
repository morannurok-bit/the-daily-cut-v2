## 1. Refined Casual (`refined_casual`)

**Thumbnail:** cream camp-collar short-sleeve shirt + dark charcoal tailored vest
(waistcoat) worn open + dark straight jeans + black leather ankle boots + small
black leather crossbody bag.

| Item | Role | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Dark tailored vest, worn open | **Hero** | The considered tailored layer makes the outfit Refined Casual. |
| Cream camp-collar shirt | Bridge | Universal refined-tier basic. |
| Dark straight jeans | Bridge | Workhorse denim across Cool/Refined/Urban. |
| Black ankle boots | Bridge | Daily casual footwear. |
| Small leather crossbody | Bridge | The "considered handbag." |

Swap the vest for a denim jacket → Cool Casual. **Hold apart from Sharp & Soft:**
Refined = *structured / considered cut / straight jean*; Sharp & Soft = *loose /
soft / natural-fabric / wide-leg*.

## 2. Cool Casual (`cool_casual`)

**Thumbnail:** navy/white raglan contrast-sleeve fitted baby tee + dark straight
jeans + clean white sneakers + sage nylon crescent crossbody bag.

| Item | Role | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Fitted contrast-sleeve tee | **Hero** | The simple fitted everyday tee carries the world — no styling event, no statement. |
| Dark straight jeans | **Hero** | Tee + straight jeans with no shaping layer is the Cool Casual signature. |
| Clean white sneakers | Bridge | Universal across casual worlds. |
| Nylon crescent crossbody | Bridge | Anti-leather, unstyled, contemporary casual. |

The tee + jeans together signal the world; either alone is just a basic.
*Watch:* the raglan contrast sleeve carries a quiet-sporty note (a Cool Tel Avivian
adjacent signal). Consider a plainer tee to avoid Q2 surface mismatch. (Open
decision #4.)

## 8. Sharp & Soft (`sharp_soft`) — *NEW*

**Thumbnail:** light-blue oversized button-down worn open + white ribbed fitted tank
+ oatmeal wide-leg cotton trouser + tan leather mule + cognac soft-leather hobo bag
+ thin gold jewelry.

| Item | Role | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Light-blue oversized button-down | **Hero** | The masculine layer *is* the world. Strip it → Cool Casual; structure it → Formal; add a graphic → Urban Ease. |
| White ribbed fitted tank | **Hero (base)** | The body-forward feminine half — femininity through *fit*, not decoration. The tension between base and layer is the language. |
| Oatmeal wide-leg cotton trouser | Bridge | The soft tailoring; carries the play into the bottom. Tonal neighbour to the shirt, not identical (avoids a monochrome blob). |
| Tan leather mule | Bridge | Quiet-luxury footwear. **Not** a thong sandal (that's Sexy) or platform (Urban). |
| Cognac soft-leather hobo bag | Bridge | Considered, quality, undecorated; warm anchor. |
| Thin gold jewelry | Bridge | Minimal; nothing competes with the line. |

Core formula: **tight basic top + wide-leg natural trouser + oversized button-down.**
Clean, tonal, natural fabric, **no decoration**. *Watch:* no dedicated core stock —
assemblable from bridges (linen shirts, wide-leg, rib tee) but the **fitted tank** is
the keystone to source.

## World Definition Chart (9 active worlds)

| World | 1. Motivation | 2. Styling | 3. Energy | 4. Style vs Comfort | 5. Cut tolerance | 6. Detail tolerance |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Cool Casual** | Simple, comfortable, unstyled | None | Genderless casual or quietly feminine | Comfort first | Body-skimming, relaxed, simple | Minimal. Function-serving details only. Decorative accents fail. |
| **Refined Casual** | Considered, mature, quality | Easy | Soft feminine, restrained | Style first, comfortable enough | Regular cuts, classic shapes; the considered layer | Refined detail — small intentional elevations. Rejects decoration-without-purpose and statement. |
| **Urban Ease** | Contemporary mix, Pinterest-aware | Some styling, composed | Gender-neutral casual | Style first | Oversized, wide-leg tailored, graphic. Rejects fitted/skinny | Contemporary statement — graphics, oversized proportions, accessory accents. |
| **Cool Tel Avivian** | Anti-fashion as statement, self-aware | Some styling | Anti-feminine or genderless, statement | Style first | Boxy, utility/athletic reference, unusual proportions | Anti-traditional — ugly-chic, utilitarian detail. Rejects conventional feminine decoration. |
| **Formal Polished** | Office-ready, considered structure | More styling | Polished feminine | Style first, then comfort | Regular structured cuts, tailored details | Tailoring detail — darts, pleats, placket/cuff, contrast stitching. Rejects decoration-without-tailoring and statement. |
| **Sexy** | Body-aware, feminine, wants to be noticed — daytime-capable | Some styling | Feminine, body-aware | Style-led | Fitted/close knits, V-necks, fitted rib, mini lengths | Body-aware feminine accents (deep V, lace trim, fitted rib). Daytime-capable. |
| **Whimsical** | Surprise elements, visual interest, dressed to be seen | More styling | Feminine with character; bold-playful | Style first | Puff sleeves, peplum, **bold** prints/stripes | Bold statement design. Keep bold (soft/pretty → Romantic). |
| **Sharp & Soft** | Masculine/feminine tailoring play; effortless-elegant | Easy-composed | Restrained, genderless-leaning; body via fit | Balanced — ease *and* polish | Fitted base **+** oversized clean layer **+** wide-leg natural trouser | Clean, undecorated; drape + tailoring do the work. Rejects lace/sheer/bell-sleeve/coastal. |
| **Romantic** | Soft prettiness; gentle femininity | Easy | Feminine-romantic, soft | Comfort-led, pretty | Soft A-line / fit-and-flare, flowy (not body-hugging) | Delicate detail — soft florals, lace/eyelet/broderie, ruffle, flutter sleeve. Rejects bold print, tailoring, body-con. |

## Boundary probes (test-only — NOT worlds)

Shown alongside the worlds to measure stretch/tolerance on one variable. No core
inventory; not matched like worlds. Governed by the seven probe rules (see the v8
world reference).

| Probe (code) | Shown | Opens → | Register (marked) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Boho lace (`bl`) | olive satin slip + cream lace trim | slip + boho-lace family; doubles as the vacation probe | feminine-romantic straddling feminine-sexy / moderate / young |
| Leopard (`lp`) | blank crop tee + leopard jeans | (A) leopard print, (B) cropped tees — two independent opens | (A) neutral / pronounced / young · (B) neutral / subtle / young |
| Parachute pants (`pp`) | fitted tank + balloon/parachute pants | balloon/parachute pants; upgrade via pairs | neutral / pronounced / classic |

**Pairs:** leopard pants + whimsical bold pants → statement bottoms generally;
parachute pants + urban_ease → trend-leaning/oversized bottoms generally.

The yellow floral dress is **not** a probe — it graduated to the Romantic world (#9).
