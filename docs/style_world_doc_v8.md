## 1. cool_casual

**Register.** Classic cuts, slim body-following silhouettes, quiet elevation,
low-key accents. Material honesty (Everlane-style transparency). The everyday
backbone — it doesn't make statements.

**Celebrates.** Considered everyday wear, quality basics with subtle details,
material honesty, body-skimming relaxed simplicity.

**Refuses.** Statement design, loud trends, oversized contemporary (that's
urban_ease).

**Core threshold.** Classic cut + quiet register + quality basic or quietly
elevated. No statement.

**Adjacent threshold.** Items from neighboring worlds that read casual-everyday
once stripped of their world signal (a refined linen shirt worn down; a wide-leg
jean that can read everyday).

**Thumbnail (current).** Navy/white raglan contrast-sleeve fitted baby tee + dark
straight jeans + white sneakers + sage nylon crescent crossbody.
*Watch:* the raglan contrast sleeve carries a quiet-sporty note — which the
framework assigns to **cool_tlv adjacent** (ringer/raglan reference). It may signal
sportier / more "statement" than the genuinely quiet cool_casual core (Cozy Rib
Tee, cigarette jean). Consider a plainer tee to avoid surface mismatch, or accept
the retro note as in-bounds. (Ties to open decision #4.)

**Inventory.** Core (9): 4, 14, 16, 21, 22, 23, 24, 27, 28. Adjacent: 2, 12, 19.
Best-stocked world.

**Gaps.** None — this is the backbone.

**Cross-world.** Bidirectional bridge with refined_casual. cool_casual items also
bridge into urban_ease (12, 19) and cool_tlv (27).

## 8. sharp_soft  *(NEW core — reactivated from parked effortless_chic)*

**Register.** The masculine/feminine **tailoring play**. A fitted, body-forward
base (feminine through the **body**, not decoration) set against masculine tailored
pieces — an oversized clean button-down, a soft vest, a wide-leg natural-fabric
trouser, a longline blazer. Tonal, clean, natural fabric, effortless. The identity
is the **tension** between the menswear layer and the body-forward base — that play
is the point, not any single garment.

**Celebrates.** Natural fabric, tonal restraint, relaxed proportion, the considered
masculine layer, body shown through **fit** (never reveal), quiet-luxury ease.

**Refuses.** Fuss, trend-chasing, loud colour/print, body-reveal-as-goal, stiff
formality, genuine sloppiness. **No decoration** — no lace, sheer, ruffle, bell
sleeve, or coastal/euro-summer flounce.

**Core threshold.** Fitted body-forward base **+** a clean masculine natural-fabric
layer or wide-leg tailored bottom, tonal and undecorated. Core formula: **tight
basic top + wide-leg natural trouser + oversized button-down overlay.**

**Adjacent threshold.** A relaxed linen shirt (layer), a tonal wide-leg (bottom), or
a fitted rib (base) used in the clean tonal way.

**Thumbnail (current).** Light-blue oversized button-down worn open + white ribbed
fitted tank + oatmeal wide-leg cotton trouser + tan leather mule + cognac
soft-leather hobo bag + thin gold jewelry. Tonal, clean, three distinct pieces
(layer / base / trouser). Reads the polished / fashion-first end of the world
(Woman 4's lane) — in-bounds.

**Register fingerprint (per-piece).**
- Light-blue oversized button-down (layer, hero) — **masculine / moderate / classic**
- White ribbed fitted tank (base, hero) — **feminine-sexy / subtle / classic**
- Oatmeal wide-leg trouser — **masculine / subtle / classic**
- Cognac hobo + tan mules — **neutral / subtle / classic**

The signature is the only world pairing **masculine-moderate (layer)** with
**feminine-sexy-subtle (base)** at classic maturity — body-forward but restrained
femininity under a menswear layer.

**Inventory.** Core: **pending CSV pass.** Candidate adjacents (assemblable from
existing stock): relaxed linen/lyocell shirts (the layer), wide-leg natural bottoms,
and the Cozy Rib Tee (the base). The **fitted rib tank** (keystone base) and a
**dedicated tonal wide-leg trouser** are the real sourcing needs.

**Gaps.** The fitted tank is the single highest-value source — base of sharp_soft,

**Cross-world.** Closest neighbor refined_casual (structured vs loose) and

## 9. romantic  *(NEW core — reactivated from parked soft_polished)*

**Register.** Feminine-romantic. Femininity through **delicate detail and soft
drape** — soft florals, lace, eyelet, broderie, ruffle, scallop, flutter/puff
sleeve, soft A-line — not through the body and not through tailoring. The clean
mirror of sharp_soft: where sharp_soft bans decoration and leads with line,
romantic bans hard line and leads with softness.

**Celebrates.** Soft ditsy/small-scale florals, lace/eyelet/broderie, ruffle,
scallop, flutter/puff sleeve, soft A-line / fit-and-flare, flowy (not body-hugging)
skirts, warm-soft palette, gentle prettiness.

**Refuses.** Masculine/tailored detail (sharp_soft), **bold** statement print
(whimsical), body-con / reveal (sexy), hard structure, satin-slip + lace-as-evening
(that reads body-led, not romantic).

**Core threshold.** Soft feminine detail **and** soft/flowy silhouette in a
low-key execution. Femininity via neckline + print + flow, never via a fitted
body-tracing cut.

**Adjacent threshold.** Soft feminine-sweet pieces that lean pretty rather than
cute-bold.

**Thumbnail (current).** Butter-yellow ditsy-floral midi dress — soft V-neck /
gathered bust, flutter sleeve, soft button-front, flowy A-line, front slit — +
delicate tan flat ankle-strap sandal + small cognac flap shoulder bag + dainty gold
jewelry. Warm-soft, low-key (the relatable romantic, not the decorated-evening
trap).

**Register fingerprint (per-piece).**
- Yellow floral flutter-sleeve A-line midi dress (hero) — **feminine-romantic /
  pronounced / classic**
- Cognac flap bag + tan flat sandals — **neutral / subtle / classic**

The dress carries the whole register; accessories stay neutral. Classic (not
girlish) — a grown-up romantic.

**Guardrails (keep the signal clean).** Keep the top **soft, not fitted** (fitted →

## Boundary probes (test-only — NOT worlds)

Probes ride alongside the shippable worlds to measure a woman's **stretch /
tolerance** on one variable. They are **not** worlds. The seven rules govern them.

### The seven probe rules (locked)

1. **What a probe is.** A single-variable boundary test measuring how far a woman's
   identity *stretches*. Not a world; no inventory; no persona. It answers "would
   she accept this one thing," not "is this her."
2. **Never matched like a world.** A probe "yes" never pulls a world's inventory and
   never contributes to the taste-axis profile. It only opens its own narrow door.
3. **On the register map, but marked.** Probes appear as dashed/outline chips so
   their unlockable register is visible, but they are **excluded from the core
   matching math.**
4. **Each probe has one written `opens →` rule**, defined before the test, never
   improvised after.
5. **Singles stay literal; only pairs widen.** One probe opens exactly its door.
   Width increases only when a defined pair fires together.
6. **Read as tolerance, not taste.** A "yes" means her identity *stretches* to
   include the thing — it widens her lane; it does not add a persona or reweight her
   core worlds. Declared ambiguity is allowed (a probe may bundle two signals on
   purpose; that ambiguity is the reason it's a probe, not a core).
7. **Graduation.** A probe that consistently lights up across the larger test
   graduates to a core world — then it earns real inventory, a full world entry, and
   joins the matching math. (The yellow floral dress already graduated this way →
   `romantic`.)

### The probe set — `opens →` rules

| Probe (code) | Shown | A "yes" opens → | Register (marked) |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Boho lace** (`bl`) | olive **satin** slip + cream lace trim | slip + boho-lace dresses & tops (one family; silhouette + lace together). Doubles as the **vacation** probe | feminine-romantic **straddling feminine-sexy** / moderate / young |
| **Leopard** (`lp`) | blank white crop tee + leopard jeans | **two independent opens:** (A) leopard print, (B) cropped tees | (A) neutral / pronounced / young · (B) neutral / subtle / young |
| **Parachute pants** (`pp`) | white fitted tank + white balloon/parachute pants | balloon/parachute pants (upgrade via pairs) | neutral / pronounced / classic |

### Pair rules

| If she picks… | …opens the wider door → |
|---|---|
| Leopard pants **+** whimsical bold pants | statement / fun bottoms generally |
| Parachute pants **+** urban_ease | trend-leaning / oversized bottoms generally |

**Notes.** Leopard is the clean probe (blank crop tee isolates the print, so its two
opens are independent). Boho lace is one family by choice (the satin-slip pulls
feminine-sexy, the lace pulls romantic — that straddle is why it stays a probe). The
parachute tank is a control, not the probe variable.

## Cross-world principles (locked)

**Style world is independent of Intent.** World = aesthetic register; Intent =
silhouette story. World tags must **not** gate intent firing.

**World commitment removed from overrides.** World matching is handled upstream at
style-Q2 thumbnail selection.

**Adjacent can carry partial commitment.** An item can have empty `core_style` with
a populated `adjacent_style` — the sharp_soft bridges will be the first live
examples once the CSV pass runs.

**Two new thumbnails run ahead of stock.** sharp_soft (no dedicated core) and
romantic (0 core) are validated *directions* with no matchable inventory yet. Either
source before launch, or accept that users who pick them get basics/adjacents only
until stock lands.

## How thumbnails drive matching

Thumbnails appear in style Q2; picks become `styles_yes` / `styles_no`.
- `styles_yes` ⊇ item's `core_style` → strong match
- `styles_yes` ⊇ item's `adjacent_style` → softer match
- `styles_no` ⊇ item's `core_style` → filter out
- neither → neutral

**At-risk thumbnails:** cool_casual (raglan reads sportier than the quiet core),
sexy (daytime thumbnail vs reveal-leaning definition), formal_polished (implies
stock the catalog doesn't hold), and now **sharp_soft** (bridge-only until the tank
is sourced) and **romantic** (0 catalog backing).
